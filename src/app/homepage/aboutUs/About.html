TumaCredo is a prodigy that came up after it was evident that organizations find themselves in surprise having spent above their budget. One of the most overlooked expenses is time which is incurred and accounted for in the financial accounts indirectly. That is if it gets a chance to be itemized. The basic essential activity for any business is to communicate. The unforgivable administrative routine is that they have to set aside time to prepare a requisition for purchasing and apportioning airtime credits for their cell phones. The period between the preparation and delivery of the ability to communicate is time energy that is not renewable. Time is a supply that cannot be changed in quantity although you can change how it is used. So we came up with a way of slowing down the time by creating an enjoyable and conscious way of spending it.

If we don’t understand how we spend our time we don’t carry any standards for how we will spend our time. We might as well sit around and watch the Kardashians all day.
