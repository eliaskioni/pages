Airtime Disbursement

FREE

Sending airtime to any of the networks in Kenya; Safaricom, Airtel, Orange is free.
The amount that you send as airtime is the amount that will be deducted from your account balance.

SMS Service

KES. 1

Each SMS is charged at KES. 1 for each 160 characters. Sending a single SMS to multiple numbers will charge KES. 1 for each number. The number of recipients and length of message(characters) determine the amount that will be deducted from your account balance.
